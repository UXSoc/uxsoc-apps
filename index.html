New Structure

Over the past few years, UXSociety as an organization has been growing at a rapid pace, this year to a grand total of 338 members, with many new projects, initiatives and clientwork. To better serve our organization, we have expanded our EB structure to best accommodate the growing vision. This year, we are introducing a new structure with Associate Vice Presidents under each Vice President Department, as well as changes to the existing departments.

Please note that the current application period is only for the Top 3 and the Vice Presidents. The AVP run will happen during Intersession.
